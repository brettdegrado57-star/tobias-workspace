🙏 Prayer Response

John 6:1–15 & Matthew 14:22–33 — Responding to God in Prayer

How to use this: After reading and discussing the passages, take time to respond to God in prayer. Work through each section below — reading the prompts, writing your own prayers in the spaces provided, and praying aloud (individually or as a group). There are no rules here — just you and God.
📘 John 6:1–15 — The Feeding of the 5,000

🙏 Thanksgiving

Thank God for a time when He provided for you in a way that seemed impossible — when the resources didn't add up but He came through anyway.

😭 Confession

Where have you said "what is that among so many?" — dismissing what little you have as too small to matter for God's work?

🛐 Supplication

What impossible need are you facing right now? Bring it to Jesus — not as a demand, but as a honest request.

✨ Renewal

Ask God to examine your heart: Why am I following Jesus? Am I following for what He can do — or for who He is?

📗 Matthew 14:22–33 — Walking on Water

🙏 Thanksgiving

Thank God for a time when He walked into your storm — when you didn't see Him coming, but He was there all along.

😭 Confession

Where have you taken your eyes off Jesus and looked at your circumstances instead? What "wave" is pulling you under right now?

🛐 Supplication

What is Jesus calling you to step out into — but fear has kept you in the boat? Ask Him for the faith to come.

✨ Renewal

When you sink, Jesus reaches out immediately. Is there an area where you need to receive His grace instead of expecting His rebuke?

✍️ Written Prayers — Make Them Your Own

😭 Confession — The "Five Loaves and Two Fish" Prayer

God, I confess that I often think what I have is too small to be useful to You...

Help me to stop comparing my resources to the size of the need...

🛐 Supplication — The "Storm on the Lake" Prayer

Father, right now I feel like I'm rowing hard against the wind...

I need You to say to me: "Be of good cheer, it is I; do not be afraid."

🙏 Thanksgiving — The "Twelve Baskets" Prayer

Lord, thank You for providing more than I need — even when I didn't see how it was possible...

Help me not to waste what You have given, but to use it faithfully...

✨ Renewal — The "Worship After the Miracle" Prayer

Jesus, when I witness Your power and provision, let me not respond like the crowds who wanted You for what You could do...

Teach me to fall at Your feet and say: "Truly, You are the Son of God."

🚶 The Peter Moment — For Group or Personal Use

A Guided Moment of Reflection

Read this slowly. Let it sink in. Then respond:

Peter was in the boat. The storm was real. The waves were crashing.
And then Jesus said: "Come."

Peter could have stayed in the boat. He could have said "not me, Lord."
But he asked to come — and he got out.

He walked. And then he sank.

And Jesus reached out His hand immediately.

What is Jesus calling you to step out of right now?

What would it look like to trust Him even if you sink?

Where in your life do you need to hear Jesus say: "O you of little faith, why did you doubt?" — and receive grace instead of judgment?

🙏 Closing Prayer — Pray This Together

Lord, we come to You with hands open — not clenched around our fear.

You see our needs before we speak. You walk into our storms before we call.
You take what seems impossible and multiply it.
You call us out of the boat and You catch us when we sink.

Give us faith that looks at You and not at the waves.
Give us worship that responds to who You are, not just what You do.
Give us the courage to step — even if we sink.

You are the Bread of Life. You are the One who walks on water.
You are the Son of God.

And we worship You.

Amen.
